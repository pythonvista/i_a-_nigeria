The Soul of a Nation

An allegorical short film about Nigerian identity. A timeless, powerful piece for history.

A man wakes up in a council chamber, bound by chains. He is surrounded by the chiefs of every major tribe. They accuse him of theft. They accuse him of betrayal.

But he is not a thief. He is the land itself. He is the history they have forgotten. He is Nigeria.
