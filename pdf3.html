THE VISION

"Until the lions have their own historians, the history of the hunt will always glorify the hunter."

I AM NIGERIA is an allegorical thriller where the nation itself is personified as a man on trial. Drawing inspiration from the visual mastery of Deakins and the narrative intensity of Nolan, we are crafting a timeless piece of cinema that speaks to the heart of African identity.

• Allegory • History • Identity

The Story

01

The Chase

A man in a torn designer suit runs for his life through a dark forest, hunted by a faceless mob. He is beaten, stripped of his wealth, and left for dead—a symbol of a nation stripped of its resources.

02

The Trial

He wakes in an ancient Council Chamber, chained before the chiefs of Nigeria's tribes. They accuse him of theft and betrayal. But as the trial unfolds, he begins to speak with the voice of history itself, recounting secrets only the land would know.

03

The Revelation

He reveals his true nature: he is not a thief, but the embodiment of Nigeria. The flag glows from within his skin, shattering his chains and the council's authority. He walks free into a new dawn.
